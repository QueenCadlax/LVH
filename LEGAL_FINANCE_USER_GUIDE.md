### 🎯 Key Things to Look For

**Professional Cards Show:**
- ✅ Professional photo (Unsplash high-quality images)
- ✅ Company/firm name (bold, easy to read)
- ✅ Specialization (what they specialize in)
- ✅ Star rating ⭐ (4.5-4.9 range for premium feel)
- ✅ Number of reviews (trust indicator: 95-156)
- ✅ Location (which Mpumalanga area)
- ✅ Verified badge ✅ (green checkmark for trust)
- ✅ Contact info (phone, email, website - clickable)
- ✅ Action buttons (Request Service, Send Message)

**Tab Content (Detail Page):**
- ✅ **Overview:** Short 2-3 line description + service area
- ✅ **Services:** Specific offerings (5 per professional)
- ✅ **Reviews:** Real testimonials with exact quotes and ratings

---

### 💡 How Filtering Works

**Step 1: Click "Service Type" dropdown**
- Choose from 13 options (Corporate, Criminal, Tax, etc.)
- Page automatically shows only matching professionals

**Step 2: Click "Location" dropdown**
- Choose from 65+ Mpumalanga areas
- Professionals update based on location selection

**Step 3: Use Search bar**
- Type professional name, firm name, or keyword
- Results filter in real-time

**Step 4: Click "Reset Filters"**
- Returns to showing all professionals
- Clears Service Type and Location selections

### 📱 Responsiveness (What Changes by Device)

**On Your Phone:**
- Filter sidebar hidden (tap ⊕ Show Filters to see it)

- Full-width search bar
- Easier to tap buttons

**On A Tablet:**
- Filter sidebar hidden (tap ⊕ Show Filters to see it)
- Cards show in 2 columns
- Good balance of width and height
- Easy to scroll

**On Desktop:**
- Filter sidebar always visible on left
- Cards show in 4 columns
- Nice spacious layout
- Sidebar sticks to top when scrolling

### ⚡ Quick Actions

**To Find a Professional:**
1. Go to Legal & Finance page
2. Optionally filter by Service Type (e.g., "Accountants")
3. Optionally filter by Location (e.g., "Mbombela")
4. Scroll through "Top Rated" or "All Professionals"
5. Click a card to see full profile

**To Contact a Professional:**
1. Click on professional card → goes to detail page
2. See contact options in right sidebar
3. Click phone number to call
4. Click email to send email
5. Click "Request Service" or "Send Message" buttons

**To Learn About Services:**
1. On detail page, click "Services" tab
2. See specific services they offer
3. Each service has a green checkmark

**To Read Reviews:**
1. On detail page, click "Reviews" tab
2. See 2-3 customer testimonials
3. Each has star rating and exact quote
