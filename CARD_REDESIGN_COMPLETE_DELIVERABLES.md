## 💡 DESIGN DECISIONS EXPLAINED

### Why White Background?
- **Premium perception:** Associated with luxury (Apple, Airbnb, Tesla)
- **Readability:** Higher contrast with dark text
- **Space feeling:** Opens up the design visually
- **Professional:** Trust and clarity signals

### Why 192px Image?
- **Better preview:** Users see what they're getting
- **Visual anchor:** Draws eyes naturally
- **Mobile friendly:** Easier to tap
- **Optimal scanning:** Perfect for card reading

### Why Minimal Badges?
- **Clarity:** Users focus on what matters
- **Design:** Less visual noise
- **Hierarchy:** Primary tier visible
- **Simplicity:** Apple aesthetic

### Why Single Hover Animation?
- **Refinement:** Apple style = minimal motion
- **Performance:** Less GPU load
- **Elegance:** Subtle > flashy
- **Professional:** Not gimmicky
