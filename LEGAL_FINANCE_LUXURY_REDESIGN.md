**Date:** May 4, 2026

---

## 🎨 Design Transformation Complete

The Legal & Finance page has been **completely redesigned to match the Health page's luxury aesthetic**. All cards, fonts, colors, layouts, and interactive elements now follow the same premium standard.

---

## 📊 Key Design Changes Applied

### 1️⃣ **Card Image Height**
- **Before:** 200px (too tall, wasted space)
- **After:** 160px (compact, efficient, luxury standard)
- **Impact:** Better visual hierarchy, more listings visible

### 2️⃣ **Card Padding & Spacing**
- **Before:** 24px (too loose, spread out)
- **After:** 14px (compact luxury, clean density)
- **Impact:** More professional, refined appearance

### 3️⃣ **Title Typography**
- **Before:** Large 18px white
- **After:** Elegant 14px gold (#C9A24D) 
- **Impact:** Luxury accent color, premium feel

### 4️⃣ **Subtitle Typography**
- **Before:** 13px gray
- **After:** 10px uppercase gray (#aaa)
- **Impact:** More refined, professional tier system

### 5️⃣ **Card Background**
- **Before:** Semi-transparent white/rgba
- **After:** Solid #1a1a1a (dark luxury)
- **Impact:** Deeper black background, more sophisticated

### 6️⃣ **Card Border**
- **Before:** Thin rgba gold
- **After:** Solid dark #333 (subtle)
- **Impact:** Modern minimalist border style

### 7️⃣ **Border Radius**
- **Before:** 8px (standard)
- **After:** 10px (refined luxury corners)
- **Impact:** Smoother, more premium curves

### 8️⃣ **Verified Badge**
- **Before:** Checkmark in circle
- **After:** "✓ VERIFIED" text badge
- **Impact:** Clear, professional, matches Health page

### 9️⃣ **Filter Panel**
- **Before:** Subtle transparent background
- **After:** Bold 2px gold border with rgba(201, 162, 77, 0.03)
- **Impact:** Clear prominence, luxury accent

### 🔟 **Section Headers**
- **Before:** Simple underline
- **After:** Left border (3px #C9A24D) + Playfair Display serif
- **Impact:** Sophisticated, premium magazine style
