## Decision-Making Checklist

**Before submitting any new copy to production:**

```
□ Does it show curation, not just listing?
□ Is it confident or defensive?
□ Is it specific (not generic)?
□ Does it tell a story (not list features)?
□ Does it align with core themes?
□ Is it repeating something said elsewhere?
□ Does it use industry-standard terminology?
□ Would an investor be impressed?
□ Does it sound professional?

## Core Principles (Reference)

### 1. **Curator, Not Catalog**
- We select. You get quality.
- Not: "Here are all businesses"
- Yes: "Here are the refined ones we selected"

### 2. **Confidence, Not Justification**
- State what you are
- Not: "We solve this problem..."
- Yes: "Built on trust. Designed for quality."

### 3. **Specific, Not Generic**
- "Most Refined" vs "Best"
- "Premium" vs "Quality"
- "Curated" vs "All"

### 4. **Narrative, Not Transaction**
- Tell a story
- Not: "Products. Sellers. Verification."
- Yes: "Discover authentic products from trusted sellers"

### 5. **Consistency, Not Randomness**
- Repeat themes ("Discover...")
- Build brand recall
- Unified voice across sections

### 6. **Professional, Not Casual**
- Hospitality vs Stays
- Healthcare vs Health
- Real Estate vs Estates

### 7. **Substance, Not Repetition**
- Each message adds value
- No repeated badges
- Unique descriptions

### 8. **Authority, Not Apology**
- Confident positioning
- Industry terminology
- Investor-ready language

## Quick Wins (Templates for Future)

### When Adding New Section Copy

**Template 1: Category Intro**
```
[Action] [Specific Descriptor] [What We Curate]

Example:
"Discover the most refined [category] in Mpumalanga, 
 hand-selected by our team of [experts]."

Application:
- Real Estate: "...most refined properties..."
- Healthcare: "...most trusted medical providers..."
- Hospitality: "...most exceptional dining experiences..."
```

**Template 2: Call to Action**
```
[Action] + [Benefit] + [Brand Promise]

Examples:
"Discover authentic experiences built on trust"
"Explore refined businesses designed for quality"
"Find trusted providers in your area"
```

**Template 3: Trust Statement**
```
[Foundation] + [Differentiator] + [Promise]

Examples:
"Built on verification. Designed for confidence. Protected by our guarantee."
"Curated with care. Verified with precision. Delivered with excellence."
```

   → "Authentic products from trusted artisans"

❌ "Quality verified"
   → "Built on trust. Verified by experts."

❌ "Browse all..."
   → "Discover our curated selection..."

❌ "See what others are missing"
   → "Discover what sets our selection apart"
```

## Testing Your Copy

### Before Publishing, Read It:

1. **Out loud** - Does it sound confident?
2. **To an investor** - Would they be impressed?
3. **Compared to competitors** - Does it differentiate?
4. **In context** - Does it flow with surrounding copy?
5. **Without it** - Would the message still be clear?
