## Strategic Framework

All changes follow 8 core principles:

1. **Curator, not Catalog** - We select, you get quality
2. **Confidence, not Justification** - State what we are
3. **Specific, not Generic** - Use precise descriptors
4. **Narrative, not Transaction** - Tell stories
5. **Consistency, not Randomness** - Repeat themes
6. **Professional, not Casual** - Industry terminology
7. **Substance, not Repetition** - Unique value
8. **Authority, not Apology** - Confident positioning
