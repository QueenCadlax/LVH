## 💡 Design Decisions

### Why Reduce Font Sizes?
- **Oversized fonts feel aggressive** → Reduced sizes feel confident
- **Apple/Airbnb use moderate sizing** → Consistency with premium brands
- **Professionalism** → Premium brands let design speak, not shout
- **Usability** → Paradoxically, smaller well-designed text is more readable

### Why Refined Borders?
- **Rounded-full feels trendy** → Rounded-lg is timeless
- **Subtle vs. bold** → Refinement requires restraint
- **Apple aesthetic** → All Apple products use minimal border radius
- **Professional** → Extreme rounding feels playful, not premium

### Why Light Typography?
- **Font-light (300) = elegance** → Bold weights feel heavy
- **Contrast with background** → Light weights pop against black
- **Digital-first** → Light fonts render beautifully on screens
- **Premium feel** → Luxury brands use thin, elegant typography
